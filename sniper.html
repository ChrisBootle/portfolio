Sniper

Unreal Engine

Brief:

In this module you will be exploring the creation of game Frameworks in engine. On completion you will have, not only the start of your own Framework, but also a stronger understanding of the Systems that go behind other projects in your various other modules. From the creation of Playable Characters, Gameplay Interactive Elements and Game Loops, you will explore the tools and processes in Engine that provide you with the ability to reverse engineer existing systems, design and then implement them into your own Game projects. On completion of the module, you will have a playable game demo that demonstrates each of the topics covered and the advanced systems within the engine. You will also understand how to approach the creation of a game from the ground up.
