Arcade

3ds Max, Adobe Substance & Unreal Engine

Brief:

For this aspect of the assignment, you will create a series of 3D environment assets for an amusement arcade within Unreal Engine.

1. An arcade machine e.g. traditional cabinet, Pinball, Air Hockey or claw crane grabber. · 1500 triangle count. · 2048 x 2048 texture space. Each 3D model should have its own basic PBR texture set: · Base Colour · Normal · Ambient Occlusion · Metallic · Roughness Or equivalent. Models should be arranged in a narratively driven and visually pleasing format for viewing.

2. A food or drinks dispenser e.g. Vending machine, Soda dispenser, Water dispenser or Gumball machine. · 1000 triangle count. · 1024 x 1024 texture space.

3. A seat e.g. bar-stool, dining-chair, high-chair or “gaming seat”. · 750 triangle count. · 1024 x 1024 texture space.

4. A soft toy e.g. Cuddly Bear, Action Figure, Plush-toy or Doll. · 500 triangle count. · 1024 x 1024 texture space.

5. Used food or drinks packaging e.g. sippy-cup, crisps packets or burger-box · 500 triangle count. · 512 x 512 texture space.
